Institute Wellness Group

We work for the betterment of the students studying at IIT Kharagpur

About Us

Under the Students' Welfare portfolio of the Technology Students' Gymkhana, IWG consists of trained students who shall enforce welfare activities among the student community and act as primary nodes of mature student counseling. Periods of life change like being in college may bring new stress and problems that have many sides and are not easily solved. We Institute Wellness Group are there to help you with such problems.

Our Work

  • 01 Work 1

    Betterment of college life for the students.

  • 02 Work 2

    Getting the students familiarize with the basic fundae of a easy and productive college life

  • 03Work 3

    Orgainising events for fun and knowledge.
